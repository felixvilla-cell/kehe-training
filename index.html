How these numbers were calculated

You asked, fair question -- the first version of this was a guess. This one isn't:

  • Video times are measured, not estimated. Loaded each file's actual metadata through the authenticated SharePoint session and read its real duration. 41 of 43 videos measured directly.
  • Two exceptions: the AOM "Comprehensive Resource Training" Session 1 & 2 (1.3-1.4GB each) wouldn't load metadata in a reasonable time, so those two are estimated from file size using the median bitrate (6.5 MB/min) observed across the 41 videos that DID measure. Real bitrate varied 2-3x between files, so treat those two specifically as rough.
  • Deck/spreadsheet times are from real slide/page/row counts -- downloaded each file and counted directly (python-pptx / PyMuPDF / openpyxl), not guessed. ~45 sec/slide, ~1 min/page, scaled for spreadsheets.
  • Note-taking buffer: the "with notes/pauses" total adds +25% on top of video time and +30% on top of reading time, since nobody watches a training video start-to-finish without pausing to write something down. That's what changed the total from the ~14.5 hours I guessed before to 41h 16m measured.
